Why Pricing Your Services Correctly is Vital

Setting your hourly rate isn't just about covering your rent. As a freelancer, you are a business. Your rate must account for taxes, health insurance, retirement contributions, and the "hidden" hours spent on admin, marketing, and sales.

Use this calculator to find your break-even point and ensure you are charging enough to sustain a healthy professional lifestyle. Remember: charging a premium rate often attracts higher-quality clients who value expertise over the lowest price.
